Motion Graphics Series

Project Overview

Created a series of engaging motion graphics for social media platforms, combining animation with brand storytelling to increase engagement and brand awareness.

The Challenge

The client needed to stand out in crowded social media feeds with eye-catching animations that effectively communicate their brand message while maintaining viewer attention in a fast-paced digital environment.

The Solution

I developed a series of motion graphics including:

  • 15-second brand story animations
  • Product feature highlight loops
  • Animated infographics
  • Social media stickers and GIFs
  • Promotional video transitions

Creative Process

The project workflow included:

  • Storyboard development
  • Style frame creation
  • Animation testing
  • Sound design integration
  • Platform-specific optimization

Tools Used

  • Adobe After Effects
  • Adobe Premiere Pro
  • Cinema 4D
  • Adobe Audition

Project Duration

6 weeks

Completed in 2024
