Social Media Campaign

Project Overview

Created a comprehensive social media campaign for a major tech product launch, including animated posts, stories, and promotional videos.

The Challenge

The client needed to create buzz and excitement around their new product launch across multiple social media platforms. The campaign needed to be cohesive yet platform-specific, engaging both existing customers and attracting new ones.

The Solution

I developed a multi-platform campaign that included:

  • Animated Instagram Stories and Reels
  • Twitter card designs and animations
  • Facebook carousel ads
  • LinkedIn promotional content
  • Custom GIFs and stickers

The Impact

The campaign achieved significant results:

  • 2M+ impressions across platforms
  • 50% increase in engagement rate
  • 200K+ shares and saves
  • Successful product launch with exceeded sales targets

Tools Used

  • Adobe After Effects
  • Adobe Premiere Pro
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Canva Pro

Project Duration

6 weeks

Completed in 2024
